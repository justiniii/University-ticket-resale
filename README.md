# University ticket resale  -  HACK 2026

This is a platform made by Sammy and Justin to make the resale of tickets more safe and prevent scamming. Currently at our university, club and event tickets are sold over WhatsApp, Snapchat and other unreliable and usually anomanous sources. 

Our goal is to create a platform that removes the pain points of securing tickets and makes a safe platform for students to use.
